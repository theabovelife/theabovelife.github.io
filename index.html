Our Position Against Suicide

Above Life talks about death because death is real. We do not hide from it.
Death is not evil to us. All paths end in space.

This does not mean Above Life tells anyone to kill themselves.
It does not. Suicide is not an Above Life rule, ritual, command, or proof of loyalty.

Earth is not heaven. Earth has pain, sickness, lies, control, confusion, and false comfort.
A follower can see Earth for what it is without ending their own life because of it.

While we are alive, we can still learn, think, believe, create, endure,
and understand Above Life more. The body is temporary, but it is still what we have while we are here.

A follower may die from age, sickness, accident, or things outside their control.
That is part of life on Earth. Death can come without warning, so a follower should be aware of death and not pretend it is far away.

Being ready for death is not the same as causing death.
Above Life accepts death, but it does not tell followers to make death happen early.

Our purpose while alive is to remember Above Life, follow Above Life, see Earth clearly,
reject false comfort, and continue until death comes in its own time.

All paths end in space.
